Welcome to BrewYourTech

Here are the available API endpoints for you to explore:

  • GET /api/phones/ - List of Phones
  • GET /api/laptops/ - List of Laptops
  • GET /api/tablets/ - List of Tablets
  • GET /api/cameras/ - List of Cameras
  • GET /api/phones/{device_id}/ - Phone Details
  • GET /api/laptops/{device_id}/ - Laptop Details
  • GET /api/tablets/{device_id}/ - Tablet Details
  • GET /api/cameras/{device_id}/ - Camera Details
  • POST /api/loginSignUp/ - Login/Signup
  • GET /api/logout/ - Logout
  • POST /api/toggle_bookmark/ - Toggle Bookmark
    (To experience this, go into brewDisplay, click the bookmark, then go back to brewLog, there you will see your bookmarked device)
